OUR STORY

Our team comes with the experience and knowledge

At Brower Electric, we strive to gain and maintain trust in our satisfied customers.

Since 1978, we have been providing our electric services all over Missouri.

Brower Electric has been passed down three generations. For over 40 years we have proved to be one of Missouri's most reliable, "go-to", Electric companies.

We are Brower Electric!

For generations, our family owned business has been solving Missouri's electric needs. We are a very flexible company, and will try our hardest to accomedate your requests. We have installed/built systems anywhere from Lighting, to Fire Alarms, to Security Systems.
